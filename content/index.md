# Welcome to My Digital Garden

This is a space where ideas grow and connect. Unlike a traditional blog, a digital garden is a living collection of notes that evolve over time.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Notes** - Fleeting thoughts and half-formed ideas
- **Essays** - More polished pieces of writing
- **Links** - Connections between different concepts

Feel free to explore by following the links between notes. You can use [[Second Note]] to navigate to related content.

## How It Works

I write notes in [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) and publish them here. Some notes are seeds, others are more developed. That's the beauty of a garden - it's always growing!
